Key Features

Fully Responsive

Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile with breakpoints at 992px, 768px, and 480px.

Auth System

Google One Tap prompt, Magic Link modal, Google SSO, localStorage session, post-login support popup.

Payment Widget

4-screen support flow: amount selection → Google Sign-In → payment methods → success certificate. Popup + embedded variants.

Cross-Page Navigation

Shared nav, footer, auth state, and support CTAs across all 10 pages.

Search & Filters

Toggle search bar in nav, product category filter dropdown, season grid.

5-Zone Monetization

Article page CTAs: Zone A (inline banner), Zone B (dark strip), Zone C (full widget), Zone D (author appeal), Zone E (sticky sidebar).
