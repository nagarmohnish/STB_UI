Beyond the Tank

Shark Tank first premiered on Hulu in 2009 and has since become one of the most popular reality TV shows in the United States. The show features aspiring entrepreneurs pitching their business ideas and products to a panel of investors, known as "Sharks," in hopes of securing funding and business partnerships.

Shark Tank Season 1 had 14 episodes, which introduced the world to the format and the original panel of Sharks. Season 2 had 9 episodes, a shorter run as the show was still finding its footing. By Season 3, the show expanded to 15 episodes as its popularity grew. Season 4 saw a significant increase with 26 episodes, reflecting the show's rising ratings and audience demand.

Seasons 5, 6, and 7 each featured 28 episodes, establishing a consistent and robust format. During Season 6, Shark Tank reached a major milestone when it aired its 100th episode, a testament to the show's enduring appeal and the fascinating entrepreneurial stories it continues to tell.

The success of Shark Tank also led to the creation of a spinoff series called Beyond the Tank, which premiered in 2015. Beyond the Tank followed up on entrepreneurs who had previously appeared on Shark Tank to see how their businesses had progressed after making deals with the Sharks.

Shark Tank Episodes on CNBC

In addition to airing new episodes on ABC, Shark Tank has been airing syndicated reruns on CNBC since January 2014. The reruns have introduced the show to an even wider audience and have helped maintain interest in the series between new seasons. CNBC typically airs episodes from earlier seasons, allowing viewers to catch up on pitches and deals they may have missed. The syndication deal has been mutually beneficial, providing CNBC with strong ratings while giving Shark Tank additional exposure and keeping the entrepreneurial spirit of the show alive year-round.

Shark Tank Season 17

Shark Tank Season 17 premiered on September 24, 2025, continuing the tradition of bringing innovative entrepreneurs and their groundbreaking ideas to the tank. Season 10 marked another milestone for the franchise when it aired its 200th episode, further cementing the show's place in television history. The show continues to evolve with new guest Sharks joining the panel alongside fan favorites, and the deals keep getting bigger and more exciting with each passing season.
