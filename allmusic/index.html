PAT METHENY
Side-Eye III+
Pat Metheny Side-Eye III+
★★★★★
User Review by AI

First ensemble studio set since 2020, combining his band with the Hollywood Studio Symphony Orchestra. The music is expansive and encompasses classics from his historical past. He layers electronics, wordless vocals, and luxuriant textural soundscapes, threading new approaches and sounds into compositions of earlier work. His guitar weds lyric harmony and rhythmic invention with emotional and atmospheric bliss. Its deft, resonant, impressionistic modern jazz with plenty of harmonic interplay between the guitarist and ensemble. The most holistic, tender, and joyful recording from the guitarist in more than a decade.
